Welcome to Your To-Do List Future!

This To-Do app is a small project utilizing the Java skills we learned throughout our semester of CSC 221. It can help anyone no matter your position in life. As an everyday adult, you will be able to map out the deadlines of the tasks your work has given to you. And those are just the most obvious examples of uses you might have for a To-Do list.
